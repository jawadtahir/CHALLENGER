There can be many valid reasons why your results may differ slightly. Reasons such as:

  • Rounding or different number types in different programming languages
  • Slightly different windowing semantics provided by the event processing framework
  • When data is partitioned, (e.g., to leverage multithreading) and the watermark is determined different within a partition
  • Edge cases in geolocation lookup, e.g., handling of cases where a point is exactly on line or if the earth curvature is taken into account. Also here some frameworks provide extensions which are slightly different.

We acknowledge that your results may be slightly off. Since the ranking is based on 5 days average those difference should nevertheless not make that huge difference in the end.

The configuration for the test is a follows: benchmark_type="test", batch_size=10k.

The AQI values are integers, first rounded to 3 digits, then multiplied by 1000. Hence the actual AQI is the value/1000.

        
benchmarkconfiguration = ch.BenchmarkConfiguration(token="checkyourprofile",
                                                   batch_size=10_000,
                                                   benchmark_name="shows_up_in_dashboard",
                                                   benchmark_type="test",
                                                   queries=[ch.BenchmarkConfiguration.Query.Q1, ch.BenchmarkConfiguration.Query.Q2])
benchmark = stub.createNewBenchmark(benchmarkconfiguration)

The snapshots are taken every batch, 10000, 20000 ... 160000
